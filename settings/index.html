Octopus Energy

Monitor your Octopus Energy tariffs, prices, usage and balance, and automate around half-hourly Agile/Go prices.

Getting started

Add an Electricity Meter or Gas Meter device. During pairing you'll be asked for:

  • Your API key — Octopus account → Personal details → Developer settings.
  • Your account number — in the form A-XXXXXXXX, shown on your bills.

Homey then discovers the meters on your account automatically.

Per-device settings

Open a meter device's Settings to change the refresh interval, VAT handling, and the cheap/expensive price thresholds used for the price level and Flow cards.

Automations

The electricity device offers Flow cards for price changes, "price drops below", price plunges, price levels, and finding/triggering on the cheapest upcoming half-hours. Both meters expose account-balance triggers and conditions.

Notifications

Choose which events should post a Homey timeline notification.

Live power

How often to refresh live power from an Octopus Home Mini. Slower cadences use less of your Octopus API allowance, which is shared with other apps.

Billing period

Set your Octopus billing day for an accurate "this billing period" summary. Leave blank to use the calendar month (lower confidence). Figures are from Octopus's settled data and update a few times a day; projections are estimates.
