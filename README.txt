Octopus Energy for Homey

Bring your Octopus Energy account into Homey: live half-hourly prices, energy usage and cost, your tariff's standing charge, and your account balance — all available as device tiles, Insights, and Flow cards.

WHAT YOU GET
- Electricity Meter and Gas Meter devices, discovered automatically from your account.
- Current unit rate and standing charge for your actual tariff (Agile, Go, fixed, variable, Economy 7 and more).
- A price level (plunge / cheap / normal / expensive) based on thresholds you choose.
- Energy usage and cost for the last 24 hours, plus a cumulative meter that appears in Homey Energy.
- Your account balance, with Insights history.

AUTOMATE WITH FLOW
- Triggers: the price changed, the price dropped below a threshold, the price went negative (plunge), the price level changed, and the cheapest half-hour started.
- Conditions: current price is below X, now is the cheapest half-hour, the price level is X, now is within the cheapest period, and the account balance is below X.
- Actions: refresh data now, and find the cheapest upcoming slot (returns its start time and average price).

Use these to run your dishwasher, EV charger, or immersion heater exactly when electricity is cheapest.

SETUP
You'll need your Octopus API key (Octopus account -> Personal details -> Developer settings) and your account number (in the form A-XXXXXXXX). Add an Electricity or Gas Meter device and enter them when prompted.

Prices are public and need no authentication; consumption and balance use your API key securely.
